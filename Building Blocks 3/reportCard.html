Report Card

Guerrilla Girls' 1986 Report Card, 1986. 56 x 43 cm (22 1/16 x 16 15/16 in.)

The unofficial official judgement of these museums and corporations in regards to their humanity and competence to understand equality and empathy as institutions.

This print is based on a poster made in 1987 in the format of a school report card. Seventeen galleries are listed with figures comparing the numbers of women exhibited in the periods 1985-6 and 1986-7. The Guerrilla Girls’ comments on their performances are handwritten in a fourth column. Most galleries maintained a consistent level; two showing a reduction and five an improvement. The source is quoted as ‘Art in America Annual 1985-6 and 1986-7’.

Tate. “'Guerrilla Girls' 1986 Report Card', Guerrilla Girls, 1986.” Tate, January 1, 1986. https://www.tate.org.uk/art/artworks/guerrilla-girls-guerrilla-girls-1986-report-card-p78808.
